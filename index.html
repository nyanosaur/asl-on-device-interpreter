Quick reference guide for hand shapes (A-Z)

GFist with index and thumb pointing horizontally parallel to each other
HIndex and middle fingers extended straight out together horizontally
IFist with only the pinky finger pointing straight up
JPinky extended straight up, trace a "J" hook shape downward in the air
KIndex and middle up in a "V", thumb pressing vertically against the middle joint
LIndex up, thumb out flat creating an "L" shape
MFist with thumb tucked completely under the index, middle, and ring fingers
NFist with thumb tucked under the index and middle fingers
OAll fingers curved down touching the thumb to make an "O" shape
PDownward pointing "K" sign; index out forward, middle down, thumb on joint
QDownward pointing "G" sign; index and thumb pointing toward the ground
RIndex and middle fingers crossed tightly over each other
SFull fist, thumb tightly folded directly across the middle fingers
TFist with the thumb tucked directly underneath the index finger
UIndex and middle fingers extended straight up, pressed together
VIndex and middle fingers extended straight up, spread apart in a "V"
WIndex, middle, and ring fingers spread straight up; thumb pinned to pinky
XFist with index finger raised and hooked forward like a claw
YThumb and pinky extended wide; middle three fingers curled flat to palm
ZIndex finger extended up, trace a "Z" pattern rapidly in the air
